Welcome to The Shire

Where Community Thrives

Step into The Shire Chamber of Commerce, where every handshake comes with a smile and every business is a cherished member of our close-knit family. Here, we're all about building bridges, not just businesses. Join us in creating a vibrant community where neighbors support neighbors, ideas spark conversations, and laughter echoes through the streets!

Business Spotlight

The Green Dragon Inn

A renowned brewery crafting fine ales, meads, and ciders with locally sourced ingredients from The Shire. With taverns across the region, it offers a warm atmosphere for enjoying hearty meals and lively entertainment.

Harvesters Co-op

A cooperative specializing in organic farming and agricultural products, providing a variety of fresh produce grown using sustainable methods. They promote community-supported agriculture (CSA) programs and farm-to-table initiatives.
